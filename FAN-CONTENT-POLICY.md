## Checklist (beta)

- [x] **Non-commercial.** No payments, ads, subscriptions, or monetization of any kind.
- [x] **Required notice shown in-app.** The About screen carries the Wizards Fan Content notice above.
- [x] **No use of Wizards logos or trademarks as our own branding.** App name/icon are generic; no MTG/WotC logos bundled.
- [x] **Card names, rules text, and imagery** come from [Scryfall](https://scryfall.com/) (bulk data + image CDN, hotlinked per Scryfall's guidelines), attributed on the About screen.
- [x] **No claim of official status.** Copy consistently says "unofficial" / "fan" where relevant.
- [x] **User data stays on-device.** The trade server relays trades and stores nothing; no accounts, no personal data collected.

## If monetization is ever considered

Re-read the Fan Content Policy first — it constrains what's allowed. Nothing in
this beta should be shipped commercially without that review (and likely legal
review, given trading + any future social/location features).
